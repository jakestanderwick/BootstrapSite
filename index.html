About Me

Who Am I?

I grew up in Idaho City, Idaho, which is a tiny town north of Boise, ID

I love being in the outdoors, but my biggest passion is technology. Anything tech related is truly what I love, and that is a large reason I am in the GIMM (Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile development) program at Boise State University. I am currently a Sophomore going into my Junior year.


Experiences

A variety of jobs have really let me see the world, as I have worked all across the Western United States

Notable jobs include being a base camp manager/clerical director for massive incidents, such as wildfires, as well as working for the labor union in Juneau, Alaska laying 16" water main down a central highway.

At this point, I have had so many experiences that I feel extremely comfortable both under pressure and when I am given large tasks.
